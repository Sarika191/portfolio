About Me

Hello! I'm Sarika Kaushal, a passionate tech enthusiast and aspiring engineer currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), currently in my pre-final year.

I specialize in problem-solving and creative development, with hands-on experience in building both fun and functional projects using C++, Java, and Python. Whether it’s developing interactive games or real-world applications, I love bringing ideas to life through code.

I’ve completed internships in content writing and graphic design, where I honed my skills in storytelling, visual creativity, and canvas-based design. I'm particularly confident in designing engaging and user-friendly digital experiences.

As an active member of the Google Developer Group (GDG), I’ve contributed to organizing hackathons, project expos, orientations, and led interviews for recruiting junior tech enthusiasts. My experiences also include Model United Nations (MUN), content writing, and receiving recognition from UNESCO for my creativity in painting.

Outside of tech, I enjoy immersing myself in anime and manga, drawing inspiration from storytelling and imaginative worlds. With a strong drive to improve and create, I strive to make a meaningful impact wherever I go.

Let’s build, express, and innovate—one idea at a time!
